# Task: Hex Wrench

Build a single-piece open-end hex wrench that fits an M10 hex bolt head.

## Requirements

Return a single **Shape** (not an assembly).

The wrench must have:
- A **handle** section for gripping (elongated, at least 100mm long)
- An **open jaw** at one end sized to fit an M10 hex bolt head (17mm across-flats)
- The jaw opening should be U-shaped or C-shaped, not a closed hole
- The whole thing must be one solid piece

## Key dimensions
- M10 hex bolt head: 17mm across-flats (flat-to-flat distance)
- Jaw clearance: 17.5mm internal width (0.5mm clearance)
- Handle: at least 100mm long, 15-25mm wide, 5-10mm thick
